About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact through education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and community service. Our mission is to inspire people to contribute towards building a better and more compassionate society.

Our Projects

🍚 Seva

Helping those in need by providing food, clothes, and essential supplies.

📚 Bachpanshala

Providing education and care to underprivileged children.

🐾 Jeev

Supporting and rescuing stray and injured animals.

👩 Udaan

Empowering women with skills, education, and employment opportunities.

🌱 Prakriti

Protecting nature through tree plantation and environmental awareness campaigns.

Our Social Impact

Through our initiatives, we strive to improve lives, protect nature, empower women, educate children, and care for animals. Every volunteer and supporter helps us build a better future.
